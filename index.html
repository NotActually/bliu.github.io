HELLO!


My name is Jason and im currently an 2nd year Software Engineering major at the University of California, Irvine. Im from Chino Hills, CA and moved there in 2015 during my junior year of highschool. My interests for technology came about during middle school after jailbreaking apple products for friends. The amount of additional features you could install blew my mind as a 7th grader. When the screen turned dark and the Cydia jailbreak script began excuting, I was sold.



After taking an introduction to C++ course in high school, writing simple code with many possibilities ingrained my love for technology. It brought me to where I am now. After participating in highschool swim team and a robotics club I found that I love belonging in a team that works well with each other.
